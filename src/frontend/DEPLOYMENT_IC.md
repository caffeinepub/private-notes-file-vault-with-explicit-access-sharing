# IC Mainnet Deployment Guide

This document describes how to deploy your application to the Internet Computer mainnet (`ic` network).

## Prerequisites

1. **Cycles wallet**: You must have a cycles wallet canister on mainnet with sufficient cycles (typically 3T+ cycles for initial deployment).
2. **dfx identity**: Your dfx identity must be configured and set as the controller of your cycles wallet.
3. **Internet connection**: Stable connection required for uploading assets to mainnet.
